- **Footer stays in place:** On dashboard pages with little content, the footer no longer floats in the middle of the screen. It now always sits flush to the bottom of the viewport.
- **Full-width layout:** The footer stretches across the entire page width, matching the WMS app layout for visual consistency.
- **No more scroll drift:** Previously the footer scrolled along with page content. It is now fixed in position so it remains visible regardless of how much content is on the page.
