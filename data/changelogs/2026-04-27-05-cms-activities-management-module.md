Added a full activities management section to the CMS admin panel. Admins can create, edit, view, publish, and unpublish activities with image uploads. Features include:

- Activity list page with search and status filtering (Draft/Published)
- Dedicated create and edit pages with image upload support
- Publish and unpublish workflow for content control
- Activity detail view page
- Activities entry in the CMS navigation menu with permission-based visibility
