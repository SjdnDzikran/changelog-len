**Eight notification templates:** The WhatsApp Templates page previously only showed two templates (one per BL type). It now displays all eight: Approval Request, Approved, Rejected, and Revision Requested for both BL Inbound and BL Outbound

**Tab-based layout:** Templates are organized under two tabs (BL Inbound and BL Outbound) instead of a single long scrollable page, making it easier to find and edit the right template

**Parallel save:** Clicking save updates all eight templates simultaneously, so there is no need to wait for one to finish before the next starts
