**WhatsApp alerts for Pickup Domestic and BL Vendor Inbound:** Approvers now receive WhatsApp messages when a Pickup Domestic Delivery or BL Vendor Inbound form needs their approval. Requesters are also notified via WhatsApp when their form is approved, rejected, or returned for revision.

**Personalized greetings:** All approval WhatsApp messages now address each approver by their real name and department group (e.g. "Budi (Warehouse)") instead of a generic "Approver" label, making it easier to identify who is being contacted in group chats.

**Customizable templates:** Admins can now edit WhatsApp message templates for BL Vendor Inbound and Pickup Domestic Delivery forms directly from the WhatsApp Templates settings page, with separate templates for approval requests, approved, rejected, and revision outcomes.
