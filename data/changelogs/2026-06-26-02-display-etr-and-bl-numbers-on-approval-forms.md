- **ETR number visible on all form types:** Users can now see the auto-generated ETR number on BL Inbound, BL Outbound, and Pickup Domestic create and view pages. The number appears as a read-only field so it is always visible but cannot be accidentally changed.
- **BL Outbound shows both reference numbers:** The BL Outbound form page now displays both the ETR number and the customer-facing BL Number, so approvers and operators can verify they are looking at the correct record at a glance.
