New form pages and dashboard shortcuts for managing Pickup Domestic shipments:

- **Pickup Domestic Inbound form:** users can fill in the BL number, material document, and inbound date, save as draft, then upload item details via an Excel template with a guided three-step process (save draft, upload Excel, submit)
- **Pickup Domestic Outbound form:** create outbound requests with outbound number, date, requester, and notes, with draft save and approval flow selection
- **Dashboard quick access:** three new buttons on the dashboard for BL Vendor Inbound, Pickup Domestic Inbound, and Pickup Domestic Outbound, so operators can jump straight to the form they need
- **Inbound detail view:** authorized approvers can fill in Origin, Moda, and AWB numbers directly in the form detail page, with inline dropdowns and Excel upload support
