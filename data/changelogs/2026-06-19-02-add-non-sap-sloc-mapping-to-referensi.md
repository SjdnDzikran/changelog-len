- **New "Non SAP SLOC Mapping" group:** A new option appears in Management > Referensi under the reference group list, allowing admins to define which storage locations should be treated as Non-SAP inventory.
- **SLOC dropdown selector:** When adding a Non-SAP SLOC mapping, admins pick a location from a dropdown instead of typing codes manually, reducing errors and ensuring only valid locations are selected.
- **Available for Network and XlHome warehouses:** Both Network and XlHome warehouses see the new mapping group, giving each warehouse independent control over its Non-SAP classification.
