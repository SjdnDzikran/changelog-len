- **Inline editing dialog:** Approvers at the final step of a BL Inbound form now see a pencil icon on each row that opens a full editable table of all items
- **Protected AWB fields:** AWB numbers are displayed as read-only badges so approvers cannot accidentally change them, while all other fields (box number, PO, part number, quantity, destination, and more) are fully editable
- **Delete unwanted items:** Items that are no longer needed can be removed directly from the dialog, and all changes are applied in a single save action
