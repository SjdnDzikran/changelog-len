A new ETR Report page under the Reporting menu lets users search, filter, and export ETR records from all form types.

- **Reporting menu entry:** ETR Report appears under the Reporting submenu alongside BL Stock Report
- **Filterable table:** Search by ETR or reference number, filter by form type (BL Inbound, BL Outbound, Pickup Domestic), status, and date range
- **Export buttons:** Each row has PDF and Excel download buttons for individual documents, plus a top-level bulk Excel export for all filtered results
- **Color-coded form types:** The Form Type column uses styled badges matching the colors used across other form tables
